# Sales Outreach Kit — Mercy AI Staff / Revora AI Employee System

A practical, copy-paste sales playbook for selling the AI Employee System
to local businesses. Use this alongside `README_DEMO_WALKTHROUGH.md` (the
demo script) and `PHASE_3_DATABASE_BLOCKER.md` (current technical status).

---

## 1. Target Niches

These six niches are the primary focus. Each has high missed-call cost,
predictable booking flows, and clear ROI from 24/7 coverage.

| Niche | Why they need it |
| --- | --- |
| **Dental offices** | High-value bookings, $300–$5,000 per case, every missed call is real money. |
| **Med spas** | Image-driven, lots of "is this right for me?" questions, perfect for AI qualification. |
| **Hair salons / braiding businesses** | Owner is on the chair, can't answer the phone, loses 30–50% of inbound. |
| **Home service contractors** (HVAC, roofing, plumbing, electrical) | Emergency calls at all hours, lead price is high, response time wins jobs. |
| **Churches** | Pastoral calls, prayer requests, event RSVPs, volunteer coordination — all 24/7. |
| **Law firms** (especially solo + small) | Every intake call is a possible case; missed call = lost retainer. |

## 2. Main Offer

> "A 24/7 AI employee team that captures leads, qualifies them, prepares
> booking/follow-up, flags missed revenue, and helps create marketing content."

Use that one sentence as the anchor. Every script below ties back to it.

---

## 3. Cold Email Script

Short, specific, no fluff. Send from a real domain. Subject lines below.

**Subject A:** `Quick question about [Business Name]`
**Subject B:** `Saw you on Google Maps — 60 seconds?`
**Subject C:** `How [Business Name] can stop missing after-hours calls`

```
Hi [First Name],

I help [niche, e.g. dental offices] in [City] stop losing
revenue from missed calls and after-hours leads.

We just built a 24/7 AI employee team for businesses like
[Business Name]. It captures every call, qualifies the lead,
prepares the booking or follow-up, and flags missed revenue
the same day — without replacing your front desk.

Could I send you a 2-minute demo video, or do a 10-minute
walkthrough this week?

[Your Name]
[Your Phone] | [Calendar Link]
```

Send rate: 30–50/day per niche. Follow up once after 3 days, once after 7.

## 4. Facebook / Instagram DM Script

Use after liking 1–2 of their recent posts. Keep it human.

```
Hey [First Name], your [recent post / new location / before-after]
caught my eye — really clean work.

Quick question: how do you handle calls and DMs when you're with
a client? Most [niche] we work with were losing 5–10 booked
appointments a month just because nobody could pick up in time.

We just built a 24/7 AI receptionist + lead qualifier for local
businesses. Want me to send a 60-second clip of it in action?
```

If yes — send the link to `/ai-employee-system` plus a 60-second screen
recording of `/demo/command-center`.

## 5. Google Maps Outreach Script

Run this against any niche with a public phone number and reviews. Call
between **10am–12pm** or **2pm–4pm** local time. If they don't answer
(which is the whole point of the pitch), follow up with the DM script
above via their Google Business Profile messages.

```
"Hi, is this [Business Name]? My name is [Your Name] with
Revora AI. I'm not a customer — I'm reaching out because I
noticed you've got [N] five-star reviews on Google but I'm
guessing you still miss calls during the day when you're busy.

We just built a 24/7 AI employee team that picks up every
call, qualifies the lead, books the appointment, and texts
you a summary. Would it be useful if I sent over a 2-minute
demo so you can see exactly how it works for a [niche] like
yours?"
```

Goal of the call: get permission to send the demo link. Do not pitch
pricing on this call.

## 6. 30-Second Phone Pitch

Use this when someone returns your call or asks "what do you do?" at
a networking event. Time yourself — it should land in 30 seconds.

```
"We build AI employees for local businesses — basically a
24/7 team that answers every call and DM, qualifies leads,
books appointments, follows up on no-shows, and flags any
missed revenue from after-hours calls.

For a [niche], that usually means 5 to 15 more booked
appointments a month without hiring another receptionist.

I'd love to show you a 10-minute demo using [Business Name]
as the example — does later this week work?"
```

## 8. Objection Responses

Keep responses short. Re-anchor on outcomes, not technology.

**"We already have a receptionist."**
> "Perfect — Mercy doesn't replace them. It picks up the calls they
> can't get to (lunch, after hours, when they're already on another
> call). Your receptionist keeps the relationships; the AI catches
> the leaks."

**"We don't need AI."**
> "Totally fair. The real question is whether you're losing booked
> appointments to missed calls. If you are — and most [niche] are
> losing 5–15 a month — this just closes that hole. The AI part is
> only how we do it."

**"How much does it cost?"**
> "Setup runs about [setup range], and ongoing support is around
> [monthly range], depending on call volume and how much marketing
> work you want it to do. Most clients see it pay for itself inside
> the first month from missed-call recovery alone. Want me to show
> you the math on your numbers?"

**"Is this safe?"**
> "Yes — and that's why we built the approvals queue. Nothing goes
> out by email, text, or social without you saying yes first. The AI
> drafts, you approve. It's a tool that protects you, not a black
> box."

**"Can it actually book appointments?"**
> "Yes. It checks your calendar, offers the open slots, confirms the
> booking, and texts the customer the confirmation. If you'd rather
> review every booking first, we flip a switch and it routes through
> the approvals queue instead. Your call."

## 9. Starter Pricing Suggestion

Use these as **starting ranges** to anchor the conversation. Adjust per
niche, per call volume, and per how much marketing automation they want.

| Component | Starter Range | What's included |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **One-time setup fee** | $1,500 – $2,500 | Discovery, agent training on the business, calendar + CRM hookup, voice setup, first round of approvals tuning. |
| **Monthly support fee** | $397 – $697 / mo | 24/7 AI receptionist + qualifier, missed-revenue dashboard, weekly performance report, included minutes pool, approvals queue. |
| **Optional premium package** | $1,497 – $2,497 / mo | Everything above, plus full marketing & social AI lane: drafted posts, review replies, outbound campaigns, monthly content review call. |

**Rules of thumb when quoting:**
- Lead with monthly first, setup second. Setup is the smaller number for
  most prospects.
- If they push back on setup, offer to roll it into month 1 + 2.
- Never quote without a 24-hour proposal in writing.
- High-volume niches (HVAC, law firms, multi-location dental) sit at the
  top of every range or above. Solo operators sit at the bottom.

## 10. Closing CTA

End every demo, every email reply, and every call with the same line.
Memorize it.

> "Would you like me to show you a 10-minute demo using your business
> as the example?"

If yes — send a calendar link immediately, before they get pulled into
something else.

If no — ask one follow-up: *"What would make this a no-brainer for you?"*
and listen. That answer is your next pitch.
