# Client assets — Revora AI Front Desk Team

**Mercy Speaks Digital**

This folder holds **polished client-facing** sales and onboarding materials for the Revora AI Front Desk Team / Mercy AI Staff product. Use it as the single place to bundle what you email, print, or hand to prospects and clients.

---

## Folder layout

| Folder | What goes here |
|--------|----------------|
| **`pdf/`** | Polished **PDF** files ready to send to prospects or clients (final layout, no accidental edits). |
| **`docx/`** | **Editable Word** documents when you need the client or team to fill in fields, comment, or co-edit before exporting to PDF. |
| **`source-md/`** | **Markdown source** copies exported from the repo (or synced here) when you want a client bundle that matches version-controlled originals. |

---

## Workflow (suggested)

1. Author or update content in the repo root (or agreed paths), usually as `.md` files.  
2. When a document is client-ready, export to **PDF** into `pdf/`, or save an editable **Word** copy into `docx/`.  
3. Optionally copy the matching `.md` into `source-md/` for a tidy package alongside exports.  

**Note:** Markdown sources in the repo are not automatically moved here—copy or export when you prepare a release for a specific client or campaign.

---

## Do not put here

- Application source code, environment files, or internal engineering notes.  
- Anything not reviewed for **client-facing** tone and accuracy.

When in doubt, see `CLIENT_DOCUMENT_PACKAGE.md` in the repo root for the full document index.
