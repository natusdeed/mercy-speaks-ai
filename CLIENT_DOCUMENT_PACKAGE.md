A simple, repeatable flow from first contact to delivery:

| 2 | `10_MINUTE_DEMO_SCRIPT.md` | Run a clean 10-minute demo of the AI Front Desk. |
| 3 | `CLIENT_SETUP_FORM.md` | Send to the client once they show interest. They fill it out. |
