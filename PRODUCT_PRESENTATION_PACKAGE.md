**Revora AI Front Desk Team / Mercy AI Staff**

Internal master deck outline + talking points for the full product pitch. Use this for partner meetings, investor / advisor conversations, mid-market prospects, and any longer-form presentation where the 10-minute demo isn't enough.

> **Internal use.** Pull slides and talking points from here. Do not send this document to clients.

## How To Use This Package

- For a **30-minute pitch:** run Sections 1 → 4 → 6 → 8 → 10
- For a **45-minute pitch:** run every section in order
- For an **investor conversation:** weight Sections 2, 7, 9, and 10 heavier
- For a **partner / referral conversation:** weight Sections 3, 6, and 10 heavier

Each section below contains:
- **Slide title** (what goes on the screen)
- **Talking points** (what you say out loud)
- **Visual / proof** (what to show)

---

## Section 1 — The Problem

**Slide title:** *Local businesses are leaking revenue every single day.*

**Talking points:**
- 30–50% of inbound calls to local businesses go unanswered.
- After-hours leads almost never convert through voicemail.
- Owners are stuck choosing between paying for a receptionist or losing leads.
- The lead they miss is the one their competitor catches.

**Visual / proof:**
- One screen-shareable stat: "A missed call to a dental office costs $300–$5,000 on average."
- Optional: a real screenshot of a busy "missed calls" log.

---

## Section 2 — Why Now

**Slide title:** *AI just crossed the line from "interesting" to "indispensable."*

**Talking points:**
- Voice quality finally sounds human.
- Setup that used to take a quarter now takes days.
- Owners no longer fear "robotic AI" — they fear missing the trend.
- The first business in a neighborhood with 24/7 AI coverage wins.

**Visual / proof:**
- Brief mention of where the industry was 18 months ago vs. today.
- One short audio clip of the AI handling a real-style call (if available).

## Section 3 — Our Solution

**Slide title:** *Revora AI Front Desk — a 24/7 AI employee team.*

**Talking points:**
- Anchor sentence: *"A 24/7 AI employee team that captures leads, qualifies them, prepares booking and follow-up, flags missed revenue, and helps create marketing content."*
- Not a chatbot. Not a voicemail. An actual team that runs in the background.
- Owner-friendly. No training required to use it.

**Visual / proof:**
- Show `/ai-employee-system` for 10 seconds.
- Move on. Don't linger — the product proves itself in Section 6.

## Section 4 — Who It's For

**Slide title:** *Built for local service businesses that lose money to missed calls.*

**Talking points:**
- Six primary niches: dental offices, med spas, hair / braiding salons, home service contractors, churches, and small law firms.
- Common pattern: high-value bookings + owner can't pick up the phone.
- Average client recovers 5–15 booked appointments per month.

**Visual / proof:**
- Niche grid (one slide, two columns, six logos / icons).

## Section 5 — How It Works

**Slide title:** *Four AI employees. One owner-friendly dashboard.*

**Talking points:**
- **Receptionist** — answers every call, every DM, 24/7.
- **Qualifier** — asks the right questions, books or routes the lead.
- **Recovery** — chases no-shows, missed calls, and dormant leads.
- **Marketing** — drafts social posts, review replies, and outbound campaigns.
- Every action passes through the owner's approval rules.

**Visual / proof:**
- Show `/demo/ai-employees` and call out each lane.

## Visual Style Notes (for the deck designer)

- Deep slate background (`#020617`), not pure black.
- Accent colors: neon cyan and electric purple.
- Generous white space — every slide should be readable in under 5 seconds.
- One idea per slide. If you need two ideas, split the slide.
- All screenshots come from the live `/demo/*` pages — never mockups.
